Klothes 4 Kids

A world where every child has access to proper clothing.


About

Ensuring every child in the Philadelphia area has access to proper clothing for school, sports, and ceremonies.

Who Are We?

Started in 2017 by Casey Bruno and Vincent Limon, two students currently enrolled at Temple University located in the heart of the North Philadelphia Community.

What Do We Do?

Klothes 4 Kids services the Philadelphia Metro area by providing access to proper clothing to every child in need. Every month, we travel to a different neighborhood/town in the Philadelphia area and visit the local school to distribute the clothing working with local school administrators.

Why We Do It?

Casey and Vincent were both influenced in their college years by the Childrens Miracle Network, and found a place where they could get involved locally and make a difference.

Donate

Ways to donate

Come to our Office!

Our office is located at 2301 N Broad Street, which is easily accessible via SEPTA Buses, the Broad Street Line, by car, and by walking. Our office is fully staffed Monday-Friday 9am - 5pm with people to take your gently used clothing.

Mail them to Us!

Fill out our Donation Form and just put it inside the box when you mail it. By filling out the donation form, we will send you a receipt in the mall so you can get your tax deduction.

Pick-Up Option!

Once a month, a Klothes 4 Kids Representative will travel throughout the Philadelphia area to pickup clothes directly from the people who either cannot bring them to the office or mail them to the office. Simply call our offices to schedule a pickup time.
